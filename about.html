About Brightling Hotel

Our hotel is in New York City with an outstanding view of the city and surrounding area. Nestled between most of the famous sites and museums.

Travel the NYC trains, buses, taxis and ferries, see the city in many different ways. Experience the exhilarating taste, smells and seasons of the city.

What Our Guests Say

We had a great time at this location. Let’s list: Pros: -Extremely clean. Hallways, rooms, pool, and lobby areas -Room was very modern and clean. Nicely decorated -Pool was a decent temperature for how hot it was! Bar staff, Spencer, Rick, Chuck, & BJ are a true asset to the Brightling Hotel brand. Honest, quick, and personable. My one and only con: There were no fresh make cookies left in the room at checking :). Other than that the Brightling Hotel was great. Thanks!

We stay at this hotel every time we get the chance. We love the pool and the restaurants. Although our favorite restaurant Fondo was closed when we were there (During Covid). Knowing that they would have limited service soon is good news. We would definitely stay there again.

Clean and relatively new property with pleasant grounds and swimming pools for both children and adults. The dining facilities on site are limited in capacity but good quality and fair value. This is a hotel that has large banquet rooms which are beautiful and is great for weddings and family gatherings. The overall atmosphere and staff are great!!
